Junke Wang 「王君可」

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in school of computer science at Fudan University, supervised by Prof. Zuxuan Wu and Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree of Computer Science at Fudan University in 2021. During my Ph.D. study, I was very fortunate to be mentored by Dongdong Chen and Yi Jiang.

My research interests lie in computer vision and deep learning, with the emphasis on training foundation models for video understanding and generation.

My work features the Omni-series, including OmniTokenizer (an image-video joint tokenizer for visual generation), OmniVid (a generative framework for universal video understanding), OmniTracker (a unified tracking model), and OmniVL (one foundation model for image-language and video-language tasks).
